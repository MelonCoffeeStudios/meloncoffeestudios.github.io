Unique Plastering and Tiling are a Norwich based company that carry out all types of, both, plastering and tiling.
Although we carry out a majority of our work in the Norwich area we are happy to travel beyond. Having over 30 years' experience no job is too small or large.

The work we carry out is of a high standard as we always have customer satisfaction at the heart of what we do.
We are also fully insured and we guarantee our work for that extra peace of mind.

Unique Plastering and Tiling have worked with an extensive list of clients both including contractors, and private customers.
We are also able to work on domestic and commercial projects.

Our plastering work includes complete re-skims, exterior rendering, fibrous plastering, and cornice fixing.
We also cover all aspects of wall and floor tiling including bathrooms and kitchens, underfloor heating, wet rooms and swimming pools.
We are able to install basic ceramics, porcelain tiles, glass mosaics, marble and much more.

Unique Plastering and Tiling will be more than happy to give you a free no obligation quotation.
So feel free to contact to arrange an appointment.
